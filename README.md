# govassist-ai
AI assistant for government documents

---

## Summary

GovAssist AI is a chatbot-based assistant that helps people understand and apply for government documents and schemes such as income certificates, death certificates, and scholarships. It guides users step-by-step, reduces confusion, and helps avoid common application errors.

---

## Background

Many people struggle with government processes because instructions are often complex and unclear. This leads to delays, rejected applications, and dependence on middlemen.

This problem is common among students, rural users, and first-time internet users.

My motivation for this project is to make government services easier to access for everyone using AI support.

Problems addressed:
* Confusion in filling government forms
* Lack of clear document requirements
* Difficulty in tracking applications
* Errors leading to rejection or delays

---

## How is it used?

Users interact with GovAssist AI through a mobile app or chatbot interface.

Steps:
1. User selects a service (example: income certificate)
2. AI asks simple questions step-by-step
3. AI explains required documents
4. AI guides form filling process
5. AI helps track application status and explains errors

Target users:
* Students
* Citizens applying for certificates
* Rural users with limited digital literacy
* CSC operators

The system is designed to work in simple language (Hindi, Marathi, English).

---

## Data and AI methods

Data sources:
* Government websites and e-governance portals
* Public scheme documentation
* FAQs and application guidelines

AI methods:
* Natural Language Processing (NLP) for user queries
* Chatbot models for conversation flow
* Text classification for identifying document type
* Rule-based decision system for step-by-step guidance
* Optional speech-to-text for voice input

---

## Challenges

Limitations:
* Cannot guarantee approval of government applications
* Depends on accuracy of government information
* Needs regular updates when rules change

Ethical considerations:
* Must ensure correct guidance to avoid wrong submissions
* Must protect user privacy
